Low-Markup Medications

We help you pay closer to the real price—without coupon games or hidden spreads.

What we look for

  • Transparent cash prices (PBM-free or clearly posted)
  • 90-day mail & warehouse unit-cost comparisons
  • Generics/equivalents to avoid brand markups (with prescriber OK)
  • Manufacturer assistance on select brand meds

Quick method (5 steps)

  1. List drug, strength, and quantity exactly.
  2. Pull 2 local cash prices + 1 mail/warehouse price.
  3. Ask prescriber about generic/therapeutic equivalent.
  4. Pick the best reliable option; calendar a re-check.
  5. Use Maisage to sanity-check or find a better path.
